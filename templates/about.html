💧 AquaFix

Smart Water Complaint Management System

🌍 About the Project

AquaFix is a smart web-based platform designed to simplify the reporting and management of water-related issues. Citizens can report problems such as water leakage, low pressure, dirty water, and supply interruptions while maintenance teams receive and resolve complaints efficiently.

🎯 Project Objectives

  • Enable quick reporting of water-related complaints.
  • Automatically assign the nearest maintenance team.
  • Improve response time and service quality.
  • Provide transparent complaint tracking.
  • Collect citizen feedback after resolution.

🚀 Key Features

  • Water complaint registration portal
  • GPS-based location detection

  • Admin management dashboard
  • Interactive complaint map
  • Feedback and rating system
  • Complaint status tracking

🎯 Sustainable Development Goal (SDG 6)

AquaFix supports the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 6: Clean Water and Sanitation. By enabling efficient reporting and management of water issues, AquaFix contributes to better water accessibility, quality, and resource management.
